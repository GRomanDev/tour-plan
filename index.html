Ekaterina Nazdorovie

Stayed 31 Dec, 2019

First time staying at this hotel. Location is very good, among the hustle and bustle of shopping and nightlife and walking distance to the beach. Other than that, it's all cons for me.

1) The rooms are old, very tired and dated looking esp the bathroom. Even the shower diverter knob came off so we were stuck with water coming out from the shower head throughout our stay. Bathroom door not even properly fitted so unable to close properly.

2) The aircon is a huge letdown. I have never ever before set my aircon temperature to 18°C with a high fan throughout my stay. With that temperature set, we were still sweating in the room. Not even cool enough for night time. No, not okay...

3) Water from the tap smells too strong of Chlorine. I even had doubts if the water is even drinkable. In the end, I just bought some mineral water for consumption instead.

4) No privacy on the balcony. You neighbours can look into your balcony. (I stayed at the top floor)

5) Breakfast has a lot of pork all over in their spread so no good for vegetarians and Muslim tourists. Breakfast is also located at another building so you'll have to walk a little. With that being said, for the amount that I had to fork out for this stay, I will not stay here again nor will I recommend anyone to stay here. Totally not worth it. It is a disappointment and doesn't live up to its name.
